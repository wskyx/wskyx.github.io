Cancers and mannequins

Published on 2 August 2024 11:32 AM
#poetry #literature #english

Robots do
Robot things
Executing commands
Programmed before
While the caravan rides
Town to town
Now one will get
Bypassed
And the mannequins
Stare at walls
At poses
Not present in nature
Black and brown liquids
Flow on time
Cancer is
Never hungry
