Suicide note EN/PL

Published on 20 December 2025 01:57 PM

Because of life situation, that is: being thrown into a nursing home under coercion, permanent lack of a sexual partner, social ostracism so also loneliness, illness and lack of perspectives I decided to end my life at age of 39. Despite being a polyglot (I fluently speak Polish and English, a bit Russian), being an artist and a poet (I run a blog wskyx.github.io and vlepy.github.io) , being a UNIX/Linux admin, familiarity with computer programming I wasn’t allowed to work but thrown into a nursing home under coercion. So I am ending my life. My will is:
- Since I’ve converted to Islam, to be buried according to Muslim rituals (Piotr 'Makak' Szarłacki will organize the ceremony)
- To be buried along with my bag of computer hardware: MacBook, iPhone, iPad, gaming controllers, etc. (honestly I’d take them with me to the other side)
- Pass rights to my poetry and visual art to my friend Piotr 'Makak' Szarłacki
- Pass 30% of profits from rights to my art to my friend Mateusz Pawłowski from Inowrocław
- Pass 30% of profits from rights to my art to The FreeBSD Foundation
