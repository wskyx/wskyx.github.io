Mind Schema of Mirosław Leśniak

Published on 9 July 2026 11:39 AM
#article #literature #english #science
Features of human mind:

• Deduction stream: thought process, can be sharp or blurry, fast or slow, logical or abstract, precise or fallible, intense or mild; majority of population has deductive process as their leading feature. The more deductive a mind is the more active a human usually is. Less deduction probably means more passivity.
• Logic: from simple to complex
• Horizon: Curiosity of external world, how open to reality a mind is, how large variety of topics it might want to process, can be from thin to wide, from 0.1 to 1 where 1 is perfectly open horizon, they can be trending into enlargement, be stable, mine is ¾. Minds of thin horizons often reject. Thin horizons build hermetic worlds and relations. Minds of wide horizons trend to enlightenment.
• Ego: Bright or dimmed, normally humans have bright ego, it can be dimmed if we drink too much vodka for instance or while being tired.
• Minds can be liberated or dependent.
A liberated mind will rely on knowledge it possesses, a dependent mind will look for answers in external world. Can be liberated either because it has a lot of knowledge (heavy), then it will have tendencies to pass knowledge, or is ignorant, then it might be preemptively oppressive.
• An analytic mind will gather and process information in shadow then show up and release a verdict when ready.

My mind after an illness lost most deduction and logic, it relies on acts of brilliance (flashes, revelations). It never will be liberated. My horizon is set at around 3/4.

In general thin horizon is a drawback. In combination of the mind being liberated gives a not too good mind. The wider horizon is the more enlightened mind gets.

All of those characteristics can be detected just by making a conversation with another person, the analysis is about to determine what kind of deduction person has, whether they're analytic, how wide their horizon is, what quality of logic they have, whether they're mind is liberated. It can look like a frindly chit chat, most people show their features openly, usually being unaware of it.

My roomate has sharp, intense deductive stream so he's pretty active, ok logic, wide horizon so he's pretty cool to hang out with. His mind didn't liberate.

Few observations: Liberation should be a reward of a kind, for gathering enough information. If a mind liberates early, it might be considered rebelious and criminal. If a person gives more data in an answer than question required it indicates a wide horizon (since good will) and perhaps inprecise, maybe blurry deduction so low inner discipline. If the deduction is shap and precise it will use strict logic. If the question is inprecise and there is will for an answer, strict logic will be ignored and there will be abstract deduction run. If abstract logic is not an option, there will be request for precising the question.

In addition to this I propose a simple, sample test for estimating the schema of a mind:

A day in Costa Del Sol

It’s a sample test for estimating schema of a mind that is being tested. It provides enough stimulus to provoke complex, deductive reactions. In this test, a person is playing a role of a tourist going to a shore town. The person will be presented situations in the town he’ll be in and will be picking choices. The person is supposed to play through entire fictional day from morning till the late night, the tester is supposed to lead them through it. So, lets begin.

The person is going to a town Costa Del Sol for vacation, in the morning gets out of a train on a train station, it’s a hot, sunny day, the person has their luggage with cloths and a computer and smartphone. On their account there is 1000 credits. Stands in front of the town map on the train station. In the town there are three hotels, one cheap one medium and one expensive three restaurants of same price spectrum, several souvenir shops, a go-cart track where there will be a guy wanting to aggressively race and two beaches when one can swim or go fishing. The town is full of tourists. In the evening there is a goa trance festival where young people party. There is a small museum of the town Costa Del Sol where one can learn about town’s history, for instance about past wars or a plague or a fire consuming the town. There is also small hospital, fire department and a police station. There also is a small, recreational airport, a church, a mosque and a synagogue. There is also a graffiti contest going on at one of the walls in the town. On the town square there are street musicians playing music.

Events:
Around noon there will be a car accident in middle of the town, two cars will crash, two people in those cars will require medical attention. At one point, after getting back to the hotel, the person notices their luggage had been stolen. The person will be attacked also by a stray dog somewhere in an alley. In the late afternoon there will be raining a bit but it will stop before the night. There will be also two lovers asking to make them a picture.

The tester asks the person questions, describes places and situations, listens to their actions and describes reactions. The tester is supposed to estimate kind of deduction the person has (constant changing of mind might indicate blurry, innacurate deduction, creativity in actions taken shows well-groomed deduction), quality of logic (sets of multiple logical elements per action indicate complex logic), scope of the horizon (the more inquiring the person is the wider horizon seems to posses), whether they're analytic or not (an analytic mind might focus on presenting conclusions of the described world), if they wont ask much from the external, fictional world and rather rely their own it might indicate liberation.
