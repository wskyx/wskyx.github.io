The lawn

Published on 8 September 2024 02:57 PM
#poetry #literature #english

A lawn waving on wind
Full of orgasm and torment
Catches last sun rays
Of this summer
Vrooming of motors
And dogs barking
British radio
Playing on the smartphone
Taste of Golden Virginia
Fills the gap in time
A raven shows up
Slowly flies away
And the white eagle
Is the master over here
I need to ask him
To get out from this place
