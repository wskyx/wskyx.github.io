Files

Published on 11 August 2024 04:15 PM
#poetry #literature

Tens of files edited
Cable In my pocket
Brick leeches got me
We share the same socket
Chicken was for lunch
That was to be expected
The Sun successfully
Breaches the blinders
Dominik wrote today too
Three poems about people
People known to us both
After a really bad night
Let us chill now
Black and brown liquids
Will flow again in liters
Down our throats
